About Us

Khalq Welfare Society (Regd.) is a non-profit organization whose aims are to provide basic Health, Education, and Social Welfare to those who can’t afford and are needy people.

It was established in 2002 and got registered in 2009. The Fatima Tufail Medical Centre is serving since 2002. In 2008 this FTMC was reconstructed and shifted to the triple story to cater to more patients.

According to facts and figures Fatima Tufail Medical Centre has been treated more than 5,51,892 patients till now and currently also giving treatment to further patients.
